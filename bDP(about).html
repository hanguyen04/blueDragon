About this website


Focusing on the knowledge aspect of raising awareness and advocating for the issue of human trafficking, I was inspired to create a quiz as it helps people to acquire knowledge well. The purpose of the creation of this website is to have a platform for that quiz. Since this is a collaboration with Blue Dragon, all credits for pictures and resources are given to every as they were taken from the Blue Dragon website and social media. We hope you found this helpful in educating you about the topic and to soon create more projects like this!
