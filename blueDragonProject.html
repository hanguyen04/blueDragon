Our Mission


Working with Blue Dragon, we focus on the topic of human trafficking as anyone can be a victim to the tragedy, therefore, we plan to raise awareness of the signs and dangers. It is something that is not being discussed enough by society and we want to draw more attention upon it since people's livelihoods are being affected in these situations. Through this website, we want to educate more people on the topic so it gains more attention and the victims receive more help.

Human Trafficking


Being a massive problem amongst rural areas in Vietnam with 80% of victims being ethnic minorities, there need to be a larger understanding of human trafficking by others in society to prevent the problem indirectly before it happens. The magnitude of the issue can not be estimated as we do not know how many are taken away to do free-labour until they are rescued or when it is too late. When enough words are spread around, counselling and education can be provided to those vulnerable to the situation and laws can be created to ensure human rights.
